Squash Fold

Take a paper that has two layers. Pull up the top flap without touching the bottom layer. Pull the outer corner to the centre and flatten the paper.

Rabbit Ear Fold

Fold the paper from its centre towards the middle line, then repeat on the other side. Afterwards, push both creases towards the middle and squash the paper down.

Beginner Models

These beginner models teach more complicated folds which are great bridges between simple folds and intermediate models.

Crane

The crane model is made up of one square piece of paper. In Japanese Tradition, the japanese crane or orizuru (ori- (folded) and -tsuru (crane)) symbolises honor and loyalty.
